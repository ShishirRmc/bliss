# Blys Booking Guide

## How Blys Works

Blys connects you with qualified, vetted wellness professionals who come to your home, hotel, or office. No need to travel — your therapist brings everything they need for a premium experience at your location.

### Step 1: Choose Your Service
Browse our menu of services including massage (Swedish, deep tissue, sports, remedial, pregnancy), facials, wellness packages, and manicures/pedicures. Each service listing includes a description, duration options, and pricing.

### Step 2: Pick Your Date & Time
Select a date and time that works for you. Same-day bookings are often available, but we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for the best selection of therapists.

Available booking times:
- **Weekdays**: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Weekends**: 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- **Public holidays**: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM

### Step 3: Enter Your Location
Enter the address where you'd like to receive your service. This can be your home, a hotel room, your office, or any private location with enough space for the treatment.

**Space requirements:**
- **Massage**: A room large enough for a massage table (approximately 2m x 1m of clear floor space, plus room for the therapist to move around)
- **Facial**: A bed, couch, or reclining chair
- **Manicure/Pedicure**: A table and two chairs

### Step 4: Confirm & Pay
Review your booking details, add any health notes or preferences (e.g., "focus on lower back", "prefer unscented oils", "allergic to nuts"), and confirm. Payment is processed after your appointment via the payment method on file.

### Step 5: Meet Your Therapist
Your therapist will arrive at your location at the scheduled time. They'll set up their equipment, confirm your preferences, and begin your treatment. After the session, they'll pack up and leave — you don't need to do anything.

## First-Time Tips

1. **Be specific about your needs.** Use the notes field when booking to mention any areas of concern, injuries, or preferences. The more your therapist knows, the better your experience.

2. **Prepare your space.** Clear a quiet area with enough room for the treatment. Ensure the room is warm (your therapist will bring linens but a warm room makes a big difference).

3. **Turn off distractions.** Put your phone on silent, close the laptop, and let housemates know you'll be unavailable. This is your time.

4. **Communicate during the session.** Tell your therapist if the pressure is too light, too firm, or if anything is uncomfortable. A good therapist will adjust immediately.

5. **Hydrate afterwards.** Drink plenty of water after a massage to help flush out toxins released during the treatment.

## How to Choose a Therapist

After your first booking, you can browse therapist profiles that include:
- **Qualifications and specialisations** (e.g., sports massage, pregnancy massage)
- **Years of experience**
- **Client reviews and ratings**
- **Photos and bio**

You can filter by:
- Specialisation
- Rating (4+ stars, 4.5+ stars)
- Gender preference
- Availability

**Our recommendation:** For your first booking, let Blys match you with the best available therapist in your area. After that, you can request the same therapist or try someone new.

## Booking for Groups

Planning a group wellness event? Blys handles:
- **Corporate wellness days**: Bring therapists to your office for employee appreciation
- **Hens parties & celebrations**: Group massages or facials at your venue
- **Family gatherings**: Book multiple therapists for simultaneous treatments

For groups of 3 or more, use the Group Wellness Package for the best value ($95 per person for a 60-minute treatment). Contact support for groups larger than 8 for custom pricing and logistics.

## Health Considerations

Please inform your therapist if you have any of the following:
- Current injuries or chronic pain conditions
- Pregnancy (we offer specialised pregnancy massage)
- Skin conditions, allergies, or product sensitivities
- Recent surgery
- Heart conditions or blood pressure issues
- Any condition that may be affected by massage or facial treatments

Your therapist will ask about your health history before beginning the session. This information is kept confidential and is only used to ensure your safety and comfort.

## After Your Booking

1. **Rate your experience:** You'll receive a notification to rate your therapist and leave a review. This helps maintain quality and helps other clients.
2. **Rebook easily:** Tap "Book Again" on your completed booking to rebook the same service with the same therapist.
3. **Manage your bookings:** View upcoming and past bookings in the "My Bookings" section of the app.
4. **Contact support:** If anything wasn't right, reach out within 48 hours and we'll make it right.
