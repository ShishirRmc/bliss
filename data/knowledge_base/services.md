# Blys Services & Pricing Guide

## Massage Services

### Swedish Massage
A classic relaxation massage using long, flowing strokes to ease tension and promote circulation. Ideal for first-time clients or those seeking a gentle, full-body experience.

- **60 minutes**: $99
- **90 minutes**: $149
- **120 minutes**: $189

### Deep Tissue Massage
Targets deeper layers of muscle and connective tissue. Best for chronic pain, muscle knots, and recovery from physical activity. Firm pressure throughout.

- **60 minutes**: $120
- **90 minutes**: $170
- **120 minutes**: $210

### Sports Massage
Designed for active individuals and athletes. Combines deep tissue techniques with stretching to improve flexibility, reduce injury risk, and accelerate recovery.

- **60 minutes**: $130
- **90 minutes**: $180

### Remedial Massage
A therapeutic treatment targeting specific injuries or conditions. Your therapist will assess your needs and tailor the session accordingly. May include trigger point therapy, myofascial release, and joint mobilisation.

- **60 minutes**: $130
- **90 minutes**: $185

### Pregnancy Massage
Specially designed for expectant mothers. Uses gentle techniques to relieve back pain, reduce swelling, and promote relaxation. Safe for all trimesters (after the first trimester).

- **60 minutes**: $110
- **90 minutes**: $160

## Couples Massage
Two therapists arrive at your location and perform simultaneous massages in the same room. Choose any massage style from the menu above. Perfect for date nights, anniversaries, or shared relaxation.

- **60 minutes**: $220 (for two people)
- **90 minutes**: $320 (for two people)

- **75 minutes**: $120

### Hydrating Facial
Deep hydration treatment for dry or dehydrated skin. Includes a hydrating mask and intensive moisturising serum.

- **60 minutes**: $95

## Wellness Packages

### Blys Signature Package
Combines a 60-minute massage of your choice with a 60-minute classic facial. The ultimate self-care experience.

- **120 minutes**: $179

### Group Wellness Package
Book for 3 or more people. Ideal for corporate events, hens parties, or group celebrations. Each guest receives a 60-minute massage or facial.

- **Per person**: $95 (minimum 3 people)

### Monthly Membership
Subscribe to a monthly massage and save. One 60-minute massage per month with your preferred therapist.

- **Monthly**: $79/month (save $20 vs single booking)
- **Cancel anytime** with 7 days notice before your next billing date.

## Additional Information

- **Travel fee**: All prices above include travel to your location within the standard service area. A $20 surcharge applies for locations outside the standard zone.
- **Minimum booking**: 60 minutes for massage and facial services.
- **Products**: All therapists bring professional-grade products. If you have allergies or product preferences, please note them when booking.
- **Tipping**: Tips are not included in the price and are entirely optional. You can add a tip through the app after your session.
