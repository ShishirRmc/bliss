# Blys Frequently Asked Questions

## Booking

### How do I book a service?
You can book through the Blys app (available on iOS and Android) or via our website at blys.com.au. Simply select your service, choose a date and time, enter your location, and confirm. You'll be matched with an available therapist in your area.

### How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability. However, same-day bookings are often available — check the app for real-time availability in your area.

### Can I choose my therapist?
Yes! After your first booking, you can request the same therapist for future appointments. You can also browse therapist profiles, read reviews, and select your preferred provider when booking.

### Can I book for someone else?
Absolutely. When booking, you can enter a different service address and recipient name. We also offer gift vouchers that can be purchased through the app — perfect for birthdays, holidays, or just because.

### What areas do you service?
Blys operates across major cities in Australia, New Zealand, the United States, the United Kingdom, and Canada. Enter your postcode in the app to check if your area is covered.

### What if no therapists are available for my requested time?
The app will suggest the nearest available time slots. You can also enable notifications to be alerted when a therapist becomes available for your preferred time.

## During Your Appointment

### What do I need to prepare?
Not much! For massage services, have a clean, flat surface available — a bed is ideal, but your therapist will bring a professional massage table. Ensure the room is warm and quiet. For facials, have access to a bathroom nearby.

### Do therapists bring their own equipment?
Yes. All Blys therapists bring a professional massage table (for massage services), fresh linens, oils, and all necessary products. You don't need to provide anything.

### What should I wear?
Wear comfortable clothing that's easy to change out of. During a massage, you'll be draped with a sheet or towel at all times — only the area being worked on is exposed. Your comfort and modesty are always respected.

### How long does a typical appointment last?
The service duration you select (e.g., 60, 90, or 120 minutes) is the hands-on treatment time. Allow an additional 10-15 minutes for setup and pack-up.

## Payment

### How do I pay?
Payment is handled through the Blys app. You can pay with credit/debit card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay. Payment is processed after your appointment is completed.

### Are tips included?
No, tips are not included in the service price and are entirely optional. After your session, you can add a tip through the app if you'd like to recognise your therapist's work.

### Do you offer gift vouchers?
Yes! Gift vouchers are available for purchase through the app or website. They can be used for any Blys service and never expire.

## Therapists

### How are Blys therapists vetted?
All Blys therapists undergo a rigorous vetting process including:
- Verification of professional qualifications and insurance
- Background checks
- In-person skills assessments
- Ongoing performance reviews based on client feedback

### Are therapists employees of Blys?
No. Blys therapists are independent practitioners who use the Blys platform to connect with clients. They set their own availability and accept bookings through the app.

### Can I leave a review for my therapist?
Yes, and we encourage it! After each session you'll receive a prompt to rate your experience and leave a review. This feedback helps maintain service quality and helps other clients choose their therapist.

## Account & Technical

### How do I create an account?
Download the Blys app and sign up with your email address or phone number. You can also sign up through our website.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and enter your email address. We'll send you a reset link within a few minutes.

### How do I update my payment method?
Go to Settings > Payment Methods in the app. You can add, remove, or update your cards at any time.

### How do I contact Blys support?

Support is available 7 days a week, 8am to 10pm AEST.
