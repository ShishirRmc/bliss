# Blys Policies

## Cancellation Policy

### Client Cancellations
- **More than 24 hours before the appointment**: Full refund. No cancellation fee.
- **Between 4 and 24 hours before the appointment**: 50% cancellation fee applies. The remaining 50% is refunded to your original payment method.
- **Less than 4 hours before the appointment**: No refund. The full booking amount is charged as a late cancellation fee to compensate the therapist for their reserved time.

### How to Cancel
You can cancel your booking through the Blys app:
1. Go to "My Bookings"
2. Select the booking you wish to cancel
3. Tap "Cancel Booking"
4. Confirm the cancellation

You will receive an email confirmation of your cancellation and any applicable refund.

### Therapist Cancellations
If your therapist cancels, Blys will immediately attempt to find a replacement therapist. If no replacement is available, you will receive a full refund. We sincerely apologise for any inconvenience — therapist cancellations are rare and taken seriously.

## Rescheduling Policy

### How to Reschedule
You can reschedule your booking through the Blys app or by contacting our support team:
1. Go to "My Bookings"
2. Select the booking you wish to reschedule
3. Tap "Reschedule"
4. Choose a new date and time
5. Confirm the reschedule request

Your reschedule request will be sent to your assigned therapist. You'll be notified once the therapist confirms the new time. If the therapist is unavailable at the new time, we'll offer alternative options including a different time or a different therapist.

### Rescheduling Deadlines
- **More than 24 hours before**: Free rescheduling. No fees.
- **Between 4 and 24 hours before**: Rescheduling is allowed but subject to therapist availability. No additional fee if the therapist confirms.
- **Less than 4 hours before**: Rescheduling is treated as a late cancellation (see Cancellation Policy above). You will need to make a new booking.

### Important Notes on Rescheduling
- You can reschedule up to 2 times per booking without penalty (as long as each reschedule is made more than 24 hours before the appointment).
- After 2 reschedules, any further changes will incur a $15 administrative fee.
- If you reschedule to a date more than 30 days from the original booking, the original booking is cancelled and a new booking is created.

## Refund Policy

### When Refunds Apply
- Cancelled bookings within the free cancellation window (more than 24 hours before)
- Therapist no-shows
- Service quality issues (reviewed on a case-by-case basis)
- Technical errors resulting in duplicate charges

### How Refunds Are Processed
Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-10 business days. You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is initiated.

### Service Quality Concerns
If you're unsatisfied with the quality of your service:
1. Contact support within 48 hours of your appointment
2. Describe the issue in detail
3. Our quality team will review your case
4. Resolution options may include: partial refund, full refund, complimentary rebooking, or account credit

We take service quality seriously and investigate every complaint thoroughly.

## Late Arrival Policy

### Client Late Arrival
If you are not ready when the therapist arrives:
- **Up to 15 minutes late**: Your session time will be reduced by the time waited. The full price still applies.
- **More than 15 minutes late**: The therapist may leave, and the booking is treated as a late cancellation (no refund).

### Therapist Late Arrival
If your therapist arrives late:
- **Up to 15 minutes late**: Your full session time is honoured. The session will end later than originally scheduled.
- **More than 15 minutes late**: You may choose to proceed with the session (full time honoured) or cancel for a full refund.

## Privacy Policy Summary

- We collect only the information necessary to provide our services: name, email, phone number, address (for service delivery), and payment information.
- Your personal data is never sold to third parties.
- Therapists only see your first name, service address, and any health notes you provide. They do not have access to your payment information or full profile.
- You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through Settings > Privacy > Delete My Account.

## Safety & Insurance

- All Blys therapists carry professional indemnity insurance.
- Blys maintains comprehensive public liability insurance covering all services booked through the platform.
- If you experience any safety concerns during a session, you can use the in-app emergency button or call our support line immediately.
